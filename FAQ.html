Where do you meet?

The BWG Coding Club meets at the BWG Public Library in the Boardroom upstairs.

When does the club meet?

The BWG Coding Club meets on the third Thursday of every month from 7:00 to 8:30 pm.

Do I have to sign up or can I just drop in?

You do not need to sign up; it is a drop-in club.

What programming languages have you learned so far?

So far, we have learned HTML, CSS, and Javascript. Please check out our 'What We Have Learned' page to learn more.

Who leads the BWG Coding Club?

The leader of the BWG Coding Club is super-coder Jake Alsemgeest.
